    ABOUT US

    JAIPRAKASH INSTITUTE OF EDUCATION AND ERUDITION (JPIEE)
    JAIPRAKASH INSTITUTE OF EDUCATION AND ERUDITION (JPIEE) is located at Dayanandnagar, Rourkela on the bank of river Brahmani. It is a dream project promoted by JPHRC. It has been approved for providing various nursing, pharmacy and allied medical science courses by the health and family welfare department, Govt. of Odisha in 2023. The institution has its own infrastructure in an independent campus with well ventilated classrooms, equipped laboratories, library, state of art auditorium and hostel for both male and female with adequate facilities. The college and hostel is insulated with well-trained security staff whereby the safety of the students is ensured.

    ABOUT JAIPRAKASH HOSPITAL AND RESEARCH CENTRE (JPHRC)

    Jaiprakash Hospital & Research Centre Pvt Ltd, Rourkela is in its fourth year of operations having opened its door for OPD patients in Oct'2019 & for IPD patients from Dec 2019.It is the only Low-Cost Multispecialty Tertiary Care Hospital in the Eastern Region. Within a very short period, it has acquired a reputation of being the fastest-growing healthcare service provider in Eastern India and the tag of being the best healthcare service provider in Western Odisha. Jaiprakash Hospital - a 307 bedded multi-specialty tertiary care health service provider - is a NABH & NABL accredited and empanelled with ESI, BSKY, FTTF, Ayushman Bharat and other major Health Insurance & TPA service providers. This ensures that any person irrespective of his/her income can avail the treatment at Jaiprakash Hospital, which is built on the premises of providing "Quality Healthcare at Affordable Price" to one and all. Besides Main Hospital at Dandiapali Rourkela, Jaiprakash Hospital also has two satellite centres at Uditnagar, Rourkela and Rajgangpur for OPD services.
