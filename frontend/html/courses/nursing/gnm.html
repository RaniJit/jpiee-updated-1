    COLLEGE OF NURSING

    General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM)

    • Course Duration : 3 Years

    • Seats Available : 60 (Government seats : 51, Management seats : 9)

      • Note : The Number of seats are subjected to change as per the availability and govt. protocol.

    • Eligibility Criteria :
      1. Candidate must be of 17 years of age as on 31.12.2023
      2. Candidate must be passed 10+2 Arts/science/commerce having minimum 40% in aggregate without extra optional 5% of total mark is relaxed for SC/ST Candidate.
      3. Candidate must have passed Odia up to M.E standard in case of Govt. institution and Govt. seat in private institutions.
      4. Candidate shall be medically fit for the course.
      5. Both male and female candidates are eligible to apply.
    • Selection Procedure : Selection of candidates is made on the basis of merit according to the mark secured in 10+2 Examination. Management quota seats are filled on the basis of merit of candidate.
